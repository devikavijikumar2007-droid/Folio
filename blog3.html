Designing a Task Management App

Date: June 2026

Designing a task management application helped me understand the process of creating user-centered digital products. I focused on simplicity, usability, and visual consistency throughout the design.

The app included features such as task creation, priority selection, task tracking, and progress monitoring. I used Figma to create wireframes, components, and interactive prototypes.

This project strengthened my UI/UX design skills and taught me the importance of organizing information clearly so users can complete their tasks efficiently.
