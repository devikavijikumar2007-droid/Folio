My Journey with Figma

Date: June 2026

My journey with Figma began when I wanted to learn UI/UX design and understand how digital products are created. At first, I learned the basics such as frames, shapes, typography, and color styles.

As I continued practicing, I started creating wireframes and prototypes. I learned how to use Auto Layout, components, and interactive flows to build professional designs.

One of my favorite projects was designing a task management application. This project helped me improve my design thinking and create interfaces that are both functional and visually appealing.
